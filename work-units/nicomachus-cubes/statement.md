# Nicomachus: sum of cubes = square of sum

## Claim
> `4 * sumcube n = (n * (n + 1)) * (n * (n + 1))`.

Nicomachus's theorem: `1^3+...+n^3 = (1+...+n)^2`. Stated without division (×4).
